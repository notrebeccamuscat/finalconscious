BECCA MUSCAT

Becca is a 23 year old artist, writer and designer based in Malta. She is currently finishing her Bachelor's in Digital Arts at the University of Malta. She likes rap music, fashion design, coffee, reading and writing. She hates the colour orange and her favourite book is Notes from Under the Floorboards by Fyodor Dostoyevsky.

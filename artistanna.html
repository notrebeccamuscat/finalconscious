Individual Exhibition

ANNA CHIARA PESSIA

Anna Chiara Pessia is a current student of BFA Digital Arts at the University of Malta. Born the 16th August of 2002 in Italy, this artist showcased her best photographs in this exhibition. She maily specializes in the illustration field but she still enjoys taking photographs of her journeys around the world. Below there are 3 of her best pieces depicting the most stricking subjects of the journey in the British Archipelago and in Oman. They were all taken during fall 2022

Soldier's Battlefield.

Taken in Oman.

Feeling Small.

Taken in Scotland.

Stairway to Heaven.

Taken in London.

Exhibition organized by Rebecca Muscat, Anisia Petruccelli and Anna Chiara Pessia, 2024.

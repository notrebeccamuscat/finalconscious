ANISIA PETRUCCELLI

Anisia Petruccelli was born in Turin, Italy where she spent most of her childhood. She then moved to Istanbul, Turkey where she spent a few years before moving to study Digital Arts at the University of Malta. Her work focuses on themes of collective consciousness, subtlety in awareness, and being.

Umbrella.

Primary colours playing with the summer light.

Lilac.

Beauty in simplicity.

Il Mercato.

A fruit market in my hometown of Turin.

Exhibition organized by Rebecca Muscat, Anisia Petruccelli and Anna Chiara Pessia, 2024.
